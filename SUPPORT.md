# Homburg Support Policy  
_Last updated: 11/26/25_

*Participating in The Homburg Foundation projects assumes you have read and agreed to the Homburg Support Policy.*

---

## 1. Purpose
The Homburg Foundation develops and maintains free and open software. This Support Policy defines standards for requesting and providing support. Anyone may request support, but governance and resolution authority is reserved for Foundation members.

## 2. Requesting Support
- Submit support requests through designated channels.  
- Provide clear, detailed descriptions of the issue, including steps to reproduce, environment details, and relevant logs.  
- Be respectful and patient; response times may vary.

## 3. Providing Support
Only Foundation members may provide official resolutions or guidance. Members must:
- Respond professionally and constructively.  
- Prioritize accuracy and clarity.  
- Maintain impartiality and transparency in all communications.

## 4. Scope
This policy applies to all Foundation-managed repositories, software, documentation, and community channels.

## 5. Enforcement
Violations of this policy may result in:
- Temporary or permanent restrictions on support access.  
- Exclusion from project activities.  
- For members: suspension or revocation of governance privileges.

Actions are determined by the severity and impact of the violation.

## 6. Amendments
The Foundation may update this Support Policy as needed. All revisions are dated and versioned.

---

By participating in The Homburg Foundation, you agree to comply with this Support Policy.
