# Homburg Code of Conduct  
_Last updated: 11/26/25_

## 1. Purpose
The Homburg Foundation supports, maintains, and develops free and open software. This Code of Conduct defines required behavioral standards. Contribution and maintenance are open to all; governance and sponsorship are reserved for Foundation members.

## 2. Expected Behavior
Participants must:
- Act with professionalism and respect.  
- Communicate clearly and without discriminatory language.  
- Provide constructive, technically grounded feedback.  
- Collaborate in good faith and avoid disruptive behavior.  

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior
Prohibited conduct includes:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.  
- Threats, intimidation, stalking, or coercive conduct.  
- Unauthorized disclosure of personal information.  
- Sexualized language or behavior.  
- Intentional disruption of project workflows or discussions.  
- Abusive or bad-faith communication.

## 4. Scope
This Code applies to all Foundation-managed repositories, communication channels, events, and any context in which an individual engages with or represents the Foundation.

## 5. Governance Responsibilities
Foundation members must:
- Exercise governance duties impartially and transparently.  
- Disclose conflicts of interest.  
- Enforce this Code consistently.  
- Demonstrate exemplary conduct.

## 6. Reporting
Reports of violations must be submitted through the reporting mechanism defined in Foundation documentation. Reports should include relevant context and evidence and will be handled confidentially.

## 7. Enforcement
Corrective actions may include:
- Warnings.  
- Temporary or permanent removal from Foundation-managed spaces.  
- Revocation of maintainer or collaborator access.  
- Exclusion from Foundation activities.  
- For members: suspension or termination.

Actions are determined by the severity and impact of the violation.

## 8. Amendments
The Foundation may update this Code as needed. All revisions will be dated and versioned.

___

By participating in The Homburg Foundation, you agree to comply with this Code of Conduct.
